Research

I'm fascinated by reinforcement learning, robotics, and memory hierarchy. To combat the high cost of data, I am particularly interested in sample-efficient techniques for learning in complex environments with sparse rewards. Additionally, I am interested in robust demonstration retrieval mechanisms for in-context learning.
